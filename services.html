Our Services

We provide comprehensive interior design and renovation solutions tailored to your needs. From conceptualization to execution, our team ensures every project is handled with excellence and professionalism.

Bespoke Furniture & Aluminum Solutions

We provide high-quality customized furniture and premium aluminum fabrication for modern Mumbai homes.

  • Aluminum Work: Sliding windows, partitions, and sleek door frames designed for durability and space optimization.
  • Custom Furniture: Handcrafted sofas, wardrobes, and modular kitchen units that blend style with 2026's "sustainable luxury" trends.

Designer Blinds & Sun Control Films

Control light and privacy with our sophisticated window treatment collection.

  • Blinds: Choose from a wide range of Venetian, Vertical, and Roller blinds to suit any aesthetic.
  • Suncontrol & Glass Films: High-performance sun control films and decorative glass films to reduce heat and add privacy to glass partitions.

Ceilings & Professional Painting

Elevate your space with contemporary ceiling designs and expert finishes.

  • False Ceilings: Specializing in Gypsum, POP, and grid ceilings with integrated 2026 "smart lighting" solutions.
  • Professional Painting: High-quality interior and exterior painting using 2026's trending earthy palettes (terracotta, olive green, and warm neutrals).

Modern Flooring & Wall Decor

Transform your surfaces with durable and aesthetic materials.

  • Flooring: Expert installation of Vinyl and PVC flooring, known for their water resistance and premium finish.
  • Wall Coverings: Premium wallpaper installation featuring 2026's favorite "textural and 3D surfaces".

MEP Services: Electrical & Plumbing

Seamless infrastructure for a hassle-free home experience.

  • Electrical Fittings: From modern concealed wiring to smart home automation and "statement lighting" installation.
  • Plumbing Solutions: Specialized in high-end bathroom fittings and efficient kitchen plumbing systems.

Specialized Repair & Maintenance

Give your old furniture a new life with our restoration services.

  • Sofa & Chair Repairing: Expert upholstery and structure repair to restore comfort and elegance to your favorite seating.
  • Furniture Maintenance & Repair: Refresh your furniture with new fabrics and finishes, tailored to your style preferences.
