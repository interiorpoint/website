Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average cost of 2BHK interior design in Mumbai?

Interior design costs for a 2BHK in Mumbai typically range between ₹4–10 lakhs depending on materials, finishes, and customization. Essential packages start around ₹3.5–5.5 lakhs, while luxury designs with premium finishes can go up to ₹20–25 lakhs+.

How do you calculate interior design fees?

We use a transparent, itemized Bill of Quantities (BOQ) model. Unlike firms that charge a flat 10–15% coordination fee, our pricing shows clear breakdowns for materials, labor, and services.

  • Full home interiors: 90–120 days

Competitors often quote similar timelines but may extend due to material delays. We commit to realistic deadlines with proactive updates.
